# Lizenzen & SDKs

- **Polar BLE SDK**: Lizenzbedingungen prüfen; Attribution im About-Screen
- **Apple Frameworks**: CoreBluetooth, SwiftUI, Combine, CoreData, PDFKit (später)
- Drittbibliotheken möglichst minimieren; Lizenzdateien im Repo halten
- **Info.plist Privacy Strings**:
  - `NSBluetoothAlwaysUsageDescription`: "LanePulse Coach uses Bluetooth to stay connected with your lane-tracking hardware and training sensors during workouts."
  - `NSBluetoothPeripheralUsageDescription`: "LanePulse Coach needs Bluetooth access to pair with your lane-tracking hardware and manage sensor connections."
